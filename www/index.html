Snake X Two

Bringing back the good old days, but with a 2-player twist!

Control your snake to collect apples and try to beat your oppontent by ultimately ending up with more apples.

Your snake will grown as your pile increases, so you will need to be more careful.

Check out the settings area for the game set up and the do's and dont's for your game. There you can also customize the rules to your liking.
